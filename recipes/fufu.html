Fufu and goat light soup

Description

Fufu is a west african dish common to Ghanaians and Nigerians. It is made by pounding cassava and sometimes yam and/or plantain.

Ingredients

    cassava
    plantain(optional)
    yam (optional)
    tomatoes
    goat
    500g diced goat or half a chicken, diced
    Half a kpakpo shito (or hot pepper)
    10g ginger
    10g garlic
    2 onions
